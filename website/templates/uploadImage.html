Recipe Recommender

Welcome to the recipe recommender! Please upload images of ingredients that you have. You will then be asked to rate a few recipes. Please rate all recipes on a scale from 1 to 5, even if you don't have the ingredients to make it. Then select either "Deep Recommend" or "Quick Recommend". Deep Recommend will use matrix factorization to produce recommendations. This may take up to 10 minutes so please be patient. Quick Recommend uses a rating frequency recommendation algorithm which will take closer to 3 minutes, use this if you only have a few ingredients.
